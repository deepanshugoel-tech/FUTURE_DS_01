Business Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard
📊 Project Overview
The Business Sales Dashboard is a data analytics project designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, profit trends, and operational efficiency using interactive visualizations. This dashboard provides business insights that help decision-makers understand sales patterns, profitability, and regional performance.

The project demonstrates practical skills in data analysis, business intelligence, and dashboard design.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales and profit performance
Identify top-performing categories and subcategories
Understand monthly sales and profit trends
Compare shipping modes and payment methods
Evaluate regional and state-wise performance
Generate actionable business insights

📁 Dataset
The dataset used in Business Sales Dataset,  which includes:
Order Date
Sales
Profit
Category & Sub-category
Customer Segment
Payment Mode
Shipping Mode
Region & State
Quantity Ordered

Region filters (Central, East, South, West)

🔍 Key Insights
Consumer segment contributes the highest share of sales.
COD payment mode dominates transactions.
Office Supplies category generates the highest revenue.
Sales peak during mid-year months indicating seasonal demand.
Standard shipping is the most commonly used delivery method.
Certain states significantly outperform others in sales contribution.
